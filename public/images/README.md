# Images Folder

Place your image assets here:

1. **Professional Portrait:** Save your high-resolution portrait as `mayank-portrait.jpg` in this folder.
   - It will automatically replace the SVG avatar on the home page.
2. **OpenGraph Social Image:** Save your brand image as `og-image.jpg` in this folder.
   - It will be displayed when you share your link on LinkedIn, Twitter, or Slack.
